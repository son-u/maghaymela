About Organizer

The Kurseong Panchayat Samiti, a dedicated administrative body, is organizing the much-anticipated Kurseong Maghey Mela 2025, a vibrant celebration of the region's culture, talent, and traditions. Nestled in the scenic hills of Darjeeling, Kurseong is renowned for its lush tea gardens, vibrant orange orchards, and breathtaking landscapes. Known as the "Land of White Orchids," Kurseong offers a harmonious blend of natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. This mela serves as a platform to bring together the diverse communities of the region and celebrate their shared identity.

The organizing committee, led by Sabhapati Smt. Anu Chettri Pradhan, a compassionate leader and dedicated social worker, aims to showcase the best of Kurseong's culture and talent. She is supported by Sahakari-Sabhapati Sri Narayan Thapa, an advocate for rural development, and a team of nine Karmadhaksya, each overseeing different aspects of the event. Their combined efforts ensure meticulous planning and execution, creating an event that reflects the unity and diversity of the region.

The Kurseong Maghey Mela 2025 is a dynamic event featuring a wide range of activities for all age groups. A Cultural Event will highlight the traditions of the hills through mesmerizing folk dances, soulful music, and traditional attire. The Mr. and Miss Kurseong (Rural) competition is a platform for the youth to showcase their confidence and personality, while the Voice of Kurseong (Rural) contest aims to discover and celebrate exceptional musical talent from the region.

For sports enthusiasts, the Beach Futsal Tournament, uniquely held by the riverside, promises an exhilarating experience that blends competition with entertainment. The mela also celebrates Kurseong's identity through events like the Orange Festival and Tea Festival, emphasizing the region's global significance in orange cultivation and tea production. Additional highlights include adventure sports, birdwatching, and a seminar on sustainable tourism, focusing on unexplored areas in the hills.

The Kurseong Maghey Mela 2025 is more than just an event; it is a celebration of the region's spirit, talent, and unity. The Kurseong Panchayat Samiti invites everyone to join this grand occasion and experience the beauty and vibrancy of Kurseong like never before.
